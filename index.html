Meet Tommy

. I am one of the candidates for Youth Representative to the National Executive committee and a youth voice activist inside and outside Labour. Some of my hobbies include politics, sailing, skiing, arts and crafts, cooking, baking, music, volunteering and youth engagement! I believe I'm the best person for this role as I have experience in representing the whole of the young people of England via a national project. I am Vice-Chair of my local BLP as well and I have represented my CLP to the north-west conference, where I seconded one motion and spoke on another both of those motions successfully passed as well. I feel as if I have got a powerful voice to engage the young people of the party and get the rest of the NEC to listen to us. Vote Tommy Kirkwood and let's make positive change happen together!

My Pledges To You


I will never be silenced

Its my promise now that nobody or any organisation will ever silence me I will always speak what I think is right and voice any injustice I find,


More representation of young people

I'll make sure I always have constant discussion with young members of the party and consult them on all decisions I make


Creation of a Young Labour online forum

I want to see the creation of an online forum for young members only where we can talk about all aspects of the Labour Party.
