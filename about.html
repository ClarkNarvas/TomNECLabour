MEET TOMMY

"Hi, I'm Tommy Kirkwood."

I am one of the candidates for Youth Representative to the National Executive committee and a youth voice activist inside and outside Labour. Some of my hobbies include politics, sailing, skiing, arts and crafts, cooking, baking, music, volunteering and youth engagement!

I believe I’m the best person for this role as I have experience in representing the whole of the young people of England via a national project. I am Vice-Chair of my local BLP as well and I have represented my CLP to the north-west conference, where I seconded one motion and spoke on another both of those motions successfully passed as well. I feel as if I have got a powerful voice to engage the young people of the party and get the rest of the NEC to listen to us.

Vote Tommy Kirkwood and let’s make positive change happen together!
